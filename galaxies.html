Introduction



We live in the Milky Way galaxy. It is a good place to live. Let's learn a bit about galaxy.








Galaxy



A galaxy is a place where lots of stars, planets are living. It turns and ends into a center which probably can be a black hole. According to the shape of a galaxy, there are 3 types of galaxies: Spiral, Elliptical and Irregular.








Milky Way



We live in the Milky Way galaxy. It is a very big galaxy which has lots of stars. Our Milky Way galaxy is a spiral galaxy . The Milky Way has a central black hole called Sagittarius A has mass four times the Sun.
